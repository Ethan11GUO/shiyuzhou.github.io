About Me

I am a master's student majoring in Materials and Chemical Engineering at the University of Science and Technology of China, expected to graduate in June 2024. Currently, I serve as a graduate research assistant at the Key Laboratory of Multifunctional Nanomaterials and Smart Systems at the Chinese Academy of Sciences, focusing on inkjet 3D printing processes. I have a background in 3D Printing, Printable Electronics, and Mechanical Design.

My research interests include functional microsystem 3D printing, wearable electronic products, and soft 3D micro-mechanics. I am dedicated to utilizing novel additive manufacturing processes for micro and nanostructures and am enthusiastic about designing, building, and observing new manufacturing processes.
